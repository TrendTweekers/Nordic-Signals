## What this is

Nordic Signals is a weekly intelligence brief on Nordic tech hiring. Every Monday morning a curated digest lands in your inbox, drawn from the public career pages of 150+ Nordic startups plus Sweden's official JobTech open-data feed.

The digest is short on purpose. Eight high-impact signals per week, each with one line of context. Not a job list.

## Why this matters

If you sell to Nordic startups, recruit for them, or invest in them, you already know the signal is hidden in the noise. The "Senior Machine Learning Engineer" posting at a company that previously had zero ML hires is the move. The "Country Head of Merchant Operations" posting at the same company is not. Aggregator job boards don't make that distinction. They show you everything in a sortable table and call it a day.

We make the distinction. A few real examples from this week's data, pulled before the digest went out:

* **Neko Health** posted a Data Science Lead. Their first dedicated data-science leadership hire. Tagged `first-ai-capability`. That's the kind of role that precedes a model-driven product expansion, not a Tableau dashboard.
* **Endra** posted a Finance Lead. Early-stage startup, senior finance hire. Tagged `fundraise-prep`. Worth a check-in if you sell tools that startups buy ahead of a raise.
* **ICEYE** posted five avionics and electronics engineers in seven days. Tagged `hardware-bet`. Not a software-only company anymore.
* **Polestar** posted a Head of Cybersecurity. Tagged `compliance-buildout`. Often precedes a regulated-market entry or scale milestone.

These are real rows from the first production run. The full week ran to several hundred relevant roles across the Nordics. The digest surfaces the eight that actually shift a worldview.

## What's in each signal

Every role in the digest carries one or more structured tags. The taxonomy was designed for VCs and dev tool reps, not job seekers:

* `first-ai-capability` — first dedicated AI or ML role at a company that previously had none
* `ai-infra-scaleup` — Staff or Principal level ML platform investment
* `fundraise-prep` — senior finance or IPO-readiness hires
* `international-expansion` — Country, Regional, or GM hires for new markets
* `hardware-bet` — robotics, embedded, or silicon hires at a software-first company
* `compliance-buildout` — senior legal or compliance, often signals regulated-market entry

The next post in this series will go deep on each tag and show what historical signals predicted, with named companies and outcomes.
